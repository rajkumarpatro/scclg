Welcome To Govt.V.Y.T.PG. Autonomous College, Durg

The College has Established in 1958 at Hindi Bhawan, Durg and Declared Autonomous College in 1989.

  • NAAC Accredited

    Our College is a First College in Chhattisgarh State Who got Grade "A" by NAAC.

  • Potential for Excellence

    First college in Chhattisgarh State, declared by UGC New Delhi as " College with Potential for Excellence, Phase- III".

  • Excellence in Science

    Our College has declared "Center of Excellence in Science" by Government of Chhattisgarh

  • SKILLED FACULTY

    The quality of our faculty is a key element of our College. Students will find great teachers in every discipline.
